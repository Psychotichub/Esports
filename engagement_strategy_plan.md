# 🏠 HOME SCREEN — Upgrade Strategy

## Objective:
Turn Home into a **content discovery engine**, not a static dashboard.

### 🔥 1. Smart Trending Engine (Not Just Carousel)

**Logic:**
Trending Score =
- (Views in last 24h × 0.4)
- + (Likes × 0.3)
- + (Completion rate × 0.2)
- + (Shares × 0.1)

**Improvements:**
- Auto-refresh every 60s
- Micro social proof (e.g., “🔥 327 watching”, “↑ 22% today”)
- Micro-preview on long press
- Soundless 2-second autoplay on scroll

**Expected Impact:** +35–50% first-video clicks

### 🧠 2. AI-Based Personalized Feed

**Track:**
- Watch time per category
- Scroll hesitation
- Skip rate
- Rewatches
- Time-of-day behavior

**Recommendation Logic:**
1. 40% Personalized
2. 30% Trending
3. 20% Similar to last watched
4. 10% Experimental discovery

Add tooltip: “Because you watched X”

**Impact:** +50–80% watch chain depth

### ▶ 3. Continue Watching (Upgraded)

- Auto-save position every 10 seconds
- Show time remaining
- Highlight unfinished videos with urgency (“7 minutes left”)
- Push notification: “Finish your tournament highlights?”

**Impact:** +45% completion rate

### 🔴 4. Live Stream Optimization

**If Live:**
- Auto-preview muted video inline
- Live chat preview snippet
- “Join 312 gamers live”

**If Offline:**
- Countdown
- “Set Reminder”
- Show best clips from last stream
- FOMO banner: “You missed yesterday’s clutch play”

**Impact:** +30% return visits
